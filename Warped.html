Duolatera

In this Virtual Reality experience, a teleportation experiment gone horribly awry maroons a scientist and an engineer in an unknown world, thus they must traverse ancient architecture using wit, teamwork, and a portal bridging mirrored realities to find their way home.
My primary role for this project is graphics programmer and technical artist. Based on concept arts, I've implemented basic cel-shader material, portal vfx material as well as several other shaders for in-game props. My non-primary role is gameplay programmer and I've helped implemented dialogue system and several other puzzle mechanics in this role.
